My Journey

My journey into Web Development started in 2017, when I was living in Melbourne Australia, having made the decision to travel upon completion of my undergraduate degree. During this period, I began to ask myself an important question: “what was next?”. At around the same time, I developed an interest in the field of technology and began to learn more about computer science, programming, and artificial intelligence.

I am proud to say that I have come a long way since beginning just a few months ago. I am now able to build professional, functional websites which adapt responsively to a range of user devices. I’m currently working with Bootstrap, Sass and Jquery, with the goal of becoming useful with Javascript.

When I’m not working, you’ll most likely find me exercising, drinking coffee or relaxing in the sun.

I’m extremely open to working on new projects, so if you have an idea, send me a message below and we can get started.
